About

The Princeton Undergraduate Law Review is an online journal that publicizes undergraduate, graduate and faculty work on law and legal issues.

Founded in 2014 and associated with the Princeton Pre-Law Society The mission of the Princeton Undergraduate Law Review is to provide a forum for the university and greater community to engage in discussion and debate about legal topics and scholarship. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact the Editor-in-Chief, Charlotte Chun, at ychun@princeton.edu.
